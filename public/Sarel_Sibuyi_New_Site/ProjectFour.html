24/7 Accommodations
A UX|UI project focused on improving the experience of communal
living of university students located in Brixton, a Johannesburg suburb.

This project explored how user experience design
can be used to help support and improve
the living experiences of university co-housing
students in Brixton Johannesburg.

24/7 Accommodations. An Honours Degree UXD Research thesis/project.
The project was showcased in the 2019 UJ FADA exhibition
amongst other student's projects.
