Project Overview

Revolutionizing Internal Processes for Home Loans

Objective:

To redesign and enhance an internal colleague platform, enabling faster and more efficient application capturing and processing. The goal was to empower internal users with multiples roles with an intuitive and robust tool to improve turnaround time and minimize errors throughout an application’s value chain.

Context:

The existing platform(Legacy) was outdated, slow, and lacked critical functionalities needed to support users effectively. This impacted our turnaround time in a market where clients got responses in 1-3days ours took about a week to two depending on the type of customer.

Challenges:

  • Misalignments between business stakeholders, designers, developers and all other stakeholders in the value chain.
  • Missed requirements, lack of accountability and different output times between discovery and delivery led to gaps in the final product.
  • The product under performed compared to its intended potential.
  • The users were hesitant to use the new platform so would quickly fall back to legacy platforms

Research and Discovery

Digging Deep to Understand User Needs

Conducted extensive user research, including interviews with colleagues who would use the platform daily.
Facilitated user testing sessions to observe how users interacted with existing tools and uncover pain points like workflow bottlenecks and usability issues.
Engaged in collaborative workshops with business and dev teams to bridge understanding gaps, aligning technical feasibility with user needs.
Engaged with MI DATA teams to understand what data they currently have and what data we could use to better understand why the colleagues where not using the new platform as intended.

Key Insights:

  • Users needed clear workflows and shortcuts for repetitive tasks.
  • Application statuses and progress visibility were critical but missing.
  • Certain workflows were overly complex, causing frustration and delays.
  • Users had a fear to participate on user testing due to a fear of feeling watched by managed.
  • Fear of change and familiarity.

Design and Prototyping

Bringing Ideas to Life

Built interactive prototypes to visualize solutions and test usability before implementation.
Created mock-ups to propose streamlined workflows and demonstrate the impact of design changes to developers and stakeholders.
Leveraged design tools like Figma to produce salable UI components aligned with the company’s design system.
Explored external tools like Jira-ticket-sync to help with improving our workflow from discovery to delivery.

Focused on improving:

  • Navigation flows for faster access to key features.
  • Data input forms to reduce user errors and improve clarity.
  • Dashboards for real-time application tracking and reporting.
